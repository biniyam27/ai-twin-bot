Using AI as a beginner is like using a calculator before you understand numbers

You'll get the answer.
But you'll never understand WHY.

Every day beginners open ChatGPT and type:
"Write me a website code."

AI writes it. They copy it. It works.
They feel like a developer.

Then someone asks them one question about their own code.
Silence. 😶

Because they didn't write it.
They don't understand it.
They just copied it.

And you cannot build a career on code you don't understand.

Learn first. Use AI later.

Spend your first 3 months writing code with your own hands.
Make the mistakes. Fix them. Understand why they happened.

That struggle is not punishment.
That struggle IS the learning.

When something finally works after you've fought for it 
that's when it sticks forever

 WHAT TO USE AI FOR INSTEAD:
✅ Ask AI to EXPLAIN a concept you don't understand
✅ Ask AI to help you UNDERSTAND an error, not just fix it
✅ Ask AI to REVIEW code you already wrote
✅ Ask AI for ideas, but YOU do the building

AI is a torch. Not a shortcut.
Use it to see better, not to avoid the road entirely.

The developers who will thrive in the next 10 years are not the ones who use AI the most.

They're the ones who understand code so well that AI becomes their superpower not their crutch.

Save this. Share it with every beginner you know. 🙏
